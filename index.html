Hello and welcome to PawMood ! Your Daily Emotional Tracker for Your Dog" This is designed to help dog owners track and monitor their dogs emotional well-being over time. By inputting your dog's mood every day, the app generates insights into their emotional state and behavior patterns. After 7 days of data collection, the app produces an average emotional profile that gives owners a deeper understanding of their Dogs mood trends. This data-driven approach helps pet owners identify any potential changes in their dog's emotional health, allowing for early intervention and improved care.
